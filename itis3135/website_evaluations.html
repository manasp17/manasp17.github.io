Website Evaluations

Userinyerface

A website known for its intentionally frustrating design that manipulates users into making errors while navigating.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast with confusing, distracting elements. Text is hard to read.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent layout and design elements, leading to frustration.
  • Alignment: Disorganized content and misaligned elements, causing visual confusion.
  • Proximity: Related items are scattered and difficult to associate, disrupting flow.

Siteinspire

A collection of beautifully designed websites that showcase excellent use of modern design principles.

  • Contrast: Excellent contrast, ensuring readability and clear distinctions.
  • Repetition: Consistent, clean layout with recurring design elements.
  • Alignment: Perfectly aligned content, providing an organized visual experience.
  • Proximity: Logical grouping of content with appropriate spacing for clarity.
